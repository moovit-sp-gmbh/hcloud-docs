# Invite members and assign roles

## Invite someone

Before you can start to build a team, invite others to your shared organization. They do not need to have a helmut.cloud account yet, but they will need to register before they can start to work.

Go to your orgainzations settings and in the left column menu, click on _Invitations_ . You can search for invitations in the list and see their status, or invite someone to your organization with the "_+ Invite user_" button in the upper right corner.

## Assign roles to members

When a person has accepted your invitation, they will be added as a "member" to your organization and will appear in the list visible via left column menu entry "_Members_".

With the drop-down in the column "Role", you can set a role for someone. A detailed explanation can be found in [Members and roles](members-and-roles.md).
