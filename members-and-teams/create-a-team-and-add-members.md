# Create a team and add members

## Create a team

In the left column menu, switch to "Teams". Create your first team by entering a name (something like "A-Team") and click on "_Create_".

## Add team members

Your team is now visible in the list and you can click on the team to view all members in the team or add members to a team.

To add members, use the "_+Add members_" button in the upper right corner. From a list of all members in your organization, you can now choose the ones you want to team. Click "_Save_" when you are done, and "_Close_" when you want to leave the dialogue.

Team members can have different roles that can be [assigned in the members section](invite-members-and-assign-roles.md).
