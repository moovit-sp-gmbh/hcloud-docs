# Spaces

Spaces are basically containers for your events and streams. They can contain multiple events and associated streams, and streams inside a space can share data or get access to passwords or tokens from the Secrets Store inside that space.

All spaces belong to an organization. Right now, one organization can have up to 100 spaces.

## View a list of all spaces

To see a list of all the spaces you are involved in, go to _High5 > Spaces._&#x20;

You will see the name of the space, its creator and the date of its creation.

## Creating a space

* In High5, go to the left side menu column and select "Spaces"
* Click "Create space" to start the wizard and choose to create a new space
* Assign a name and click okay

Your new space is ready now.

## Rename a space

To rename a space, go to _High5 > Spaces_ and hover with your cursor over the space you want to rename. Click on the pencil-icon next to the name of the space.

## Manage a spaces settings

To manage a spaces settings, go to _High5 > Spaces_ and click on the space you want to configure. You will get to the overview of the spaces properties. Click on _Settings_ and you will see the list of all configurable settings for this space.

### Wave Engine

The Wave Engine is the executing stream engine of helmut.cloud. It is under constant development. To avoid breaking your workflows and streams by forcing an auto update on your engine, you can set the wave engine version yourself when you are ready to accept and test new features or bug fixes. See the changelog for more details.

## Delete a space

To delete a space, go to _High5 > Spaces_ and hover with your cursor over the space you want to delete. Click on the X-icon next to the name of the space. You need to confirm that you want to delete the space, as it will be deleted inevitably.
