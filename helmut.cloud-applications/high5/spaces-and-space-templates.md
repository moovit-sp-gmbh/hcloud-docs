# Spaces and space templates

Spaces are basically containers for your events and streams. It makes sense to use one space for one workflow, even though a space can contain multiple events, because streams inside a container can share data.

All spaces belong to an organization. Right now, one organization can have up to 100 spaces.

## Creating spaces

* In High5, go to the left side menu column and select "Spaces"
* Click "Create space" to start the wizard and choose to create a new space
* Assign a name and click okay

Your space is ready now.

## Creating spaces from a template

* In High5, go to the left side menu column and select "Spaces"
* Click "Create space" to start the wizard and choose to create a space out of a template
* Choose the template that you want to use
* Assign a name and click okay

Your space is ready now and already filled with the items from the template.
