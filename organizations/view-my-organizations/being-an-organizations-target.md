# Being an organizations target

As a member of an organization, you can participate in executing jobs for this organization on your workstation. For this, you need to have the helmut.cloud agent software installed and running, and you need to be authorized and authenticated (meaning: logged in).

In case you do not want to execute jobs for this organization (for example, because you are a team manager or administrator), you can stop being a target by turning off the toggle switch here. Your agent will not take any more jobs from this organization after deactivating this option.&#x20;
