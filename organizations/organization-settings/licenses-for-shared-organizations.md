# Licenses for shared organizations

In helmut.cloud, licenses are divided into personal licenses (account licenses) and licenses for shared organizations.&#x20;

Each shared organization requires its own license that is part of a plan. Depending on your plan and needs, certain quota apply to your number of organization members, agents, teams and domains, as well as single properties of spaces in High5 or Fuse.

Please have a look at [helmut.cloud plans](https://helmut.cloud/) or contact our team if you need to know more about licensing option for resellers or enterprises.
