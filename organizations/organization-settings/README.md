# Organization settings

Make sure [you have selected the organization](../choosing-an-organization-to-work-in.md) you want to configure, then click on the gear wheel symbol to the left of the organization menu.

Here, you can view the organizations

* members
* invitations to other users
* teams
* license
* and logs.

To learn how to add and manage members to your org, team them and assign roles, please look at the section [Members and teams](broken-reference).
