# What are organizations in helmut.cloud?

To enable collaboration and working beyond the borders of your very own system access, privileges and licenses are managed in organizations.

That said, an organization is not necessarily depicting your real life company or team - it is simply the default structure to organize who is allowed to do what in certain environments.

That is why everyone has to be in an organization to do something in helmut.cloud. By default, you already have a private organization when you start with a fresh account. This "private org" is a safe space for your own ideas - it belongs only to you, and you cannot share anything from your private org with others.

If you want to start working with others, a (non-private) organization is mandatory. The amount of users and actions that are possible with this organization depend on your license.
