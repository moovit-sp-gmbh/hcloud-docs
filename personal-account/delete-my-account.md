# Delete my account

**Note: As of now, there is no delete your account-button. Please write us to get your account deleted.**



To delete your account, go to your _Account settings > My Profile_ and choose _Delete my account_.

You need to confirm this action. After confirmation, your account will be deleted for good and without any option to restore your data.

**⚠️ You cannot delete your account if you are the sole owner of one or more organizations. Please appoint a new owner for all those organizations first, then delete your account.**
