# Account settings

To view your personal account settings and data, navigate to your avatar in the upper right corner and click on the drop down menu > _Account settings_.

Your personal account is always connected to your private organization - the one always comes with the other. That means, you will also be lead to your account settings by choosing "My private org" in the drop down menu right next to your account avatar, and then clicking on the gear wheel symbol.

In your account settings, you can choose between viewing:

* Your profile
* Security settings
  * Password & 2FA
  * Personal Access Token (PAT)
* Notifications
* License
* Logs
