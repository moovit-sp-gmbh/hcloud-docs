# My profile

The "My profile" sections contains

* Your avatar or profile picture
* Your name and company
* Your e-mail address
* The timezone you want to work in
* The date / time format you want to use in helmut.cloud

The timezone and date / time format that you can select here will only have influence on how YOU VIEW timestamps with your personal account in helmut.cloud. As this is dynamic, it can be changed at any time to suit your needs. Timestamps will not be changed by setting different options here.
