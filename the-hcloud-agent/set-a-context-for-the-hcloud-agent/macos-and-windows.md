# MacOS and Windows

Right-click the H-icon in your taskbar and choose "_Configure_". A website opens in your browser. By default, no context is defined. To add context, click on "_Create context_" and log in with your account or [PAT (Personal Access Token)](../../personal-account/account-settings/security.md).

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/TestStream_connect_agent_nocontext_20240326.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>"+Create context" prompts you with the choice for a login screen or PAT input</p></figcaption></figure>

Depending on the settings of your private or shared organization, your agent will inherit the rights to act (or not act) accordingly.

You are now ready to use the agent on your workstation.
