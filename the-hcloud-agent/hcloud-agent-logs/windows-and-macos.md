# Windows and MacOS

To read the HCloud agents logfiles, do a right-click on the HC-Icon and choose "_Logs_". You will be lead to the logs rightaway, called _"agent.log_".

With the log, you will be abled to view the events that are executed on your machine, to supervise the connection to helmut.cloud and to see errors when they appear.

If you experience problems with HCloud or some workflows and modules and you want to address support to solve them, please include the agent.log into your ticket, accompanied by a description of the problem and the time it appeared.
