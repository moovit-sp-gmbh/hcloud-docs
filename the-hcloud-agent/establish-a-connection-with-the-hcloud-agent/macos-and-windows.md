# MacOS and Windows

Right-click the H-icon in your taskbar and choose "_Configure_". A website opens in your browser. By default, there are no connections. To add a connection, click on "_Connect_" and log in with your account or [PAT (Personal Access Token)](../../personal-account/account-settings/security.md).

Multiple connections are possible.

Depending on the settings of your private or shared organization, your agent will inherit the rights to act (or not act) accordingly.

You are now ready to use the agent on your workstation.
