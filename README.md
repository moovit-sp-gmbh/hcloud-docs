# Introduction

Welcome, traveller. You have found your way to helmut.cloud, a brand new product for media professionals. helmut.cloud is revolutionizing the way of interconnecting applications, and it has come to break data isolation and build new workflows beyond an apps original scope.

helmut.cloud brings user-friendly UIs and a set of media-specific applications. This way, it enables out-of-the-box users to expand workflows over different applications, using the latest web technologies. For specialists, helmut.cloud offers even deeper integration up to the level of writing your own code.



**Who is helmut.cloud for?**

In first instance, helmut.cloud is made for media professionals. The company behind helmut.cloud has had the chance to draw experience from years of development and systems integration with Helmut4, the on-premise version and predecessor of Helmut. So we made helmut.cloud with media professionals in mind, and as a media professional you will find a lot of tools that are familiar to you.

But helmut.cloud also is data agnostic, so it does not end here. We have discovered the need of many of our clients to go beyond the scope of specialized media software. The options how to use helmut.cloud are literally innumerable: You decide what data to use, and how.

If you are a technical specialist, an administrator of a postproduction or a broadcast environment, or a systems integrator, you will quickly find the solutions for some of the challenges of your daily business here.



**The challenge: specialized software and data isolation**

We all use specialized applications to fulfill our daily tasks - one for editing, one for media management, one for spreadsheets and another one for media review. And while that one website is very good for the process of reviewing your videoclips, it is yet another website you subscribed to for the reason of a single task.&#x20;

The initial strength of an application - its specialization - is now a challenge. The scope of the software may not be designed to fit your needs entirely, and eventually you are forced to change your workflow or adapt by manually pushing data from app A to app B. The cost is your teams valuable time to make it work and get over the problem of isolated data pools in so many of your applications.



**The solution: flexible interfaces, easy configuration and collaboration**

helmut.cloud is about to change that: It offers a user interface with a variety of smart and easily configurable ways to establish a technical connection between applications, share their data, make a workflow out of it and automate the hideous, repeating tasks. It is cloud-based and it will be there when you need it, where you need it.

Collaborate with your team, your company or the company you are sharing a project with: You decide what workflows will be shared, and who will have access to the data. If you do not want to share anything, use helmut.cloud only for yourself.

With Helmut4, we could collect a lot of experience with systems integration and workflow management for media professionals and media companies. The next logical step for us was to bring Helmut into the cloud and by that, beyond the borders of on-prem systems.
