## High5

**High5** is the heart of helmut.cloud. High5 helps you to make connections between applications - no matter what, as long as they support some interface for automation (webhooks or a Rest API for example). It also gives you the power to control who can use your workflows.

High5 allows you to

* manage your event triggers and workflows by organizing them in spaces,
* define variables and secrets that can be used inside a space,
* orchestrate events and actions graphically within the Stream Designer Studio,
* administrate the agents that execute your actions for you,
* and monitor your workflows in a dashboard.

### Spaces in High5

Spaces are buckets for everything that has to do with your workflows. They are not very specific in what they can contain - except that one space is for one workflow. _(Yes, every rule has exceptions, but we will come back to that at a later stage)._

**What you need to know for now:** Spaces allow you to manage almost everything about a workflow.

### Stream Designer Studio

The Stream Designer Studio is an applications that enables you to design your workflows visually. Using the Stream Designer Studio to build a stream is basically like graphical programming. You can choose between a variety of nodes that represent single actions, conditions or event triggers. Drag them onto your canvas and connect them in the order you need them to be executed.

With the Stream Designer Studio, you can build low-code streams (that is how we call this part of our workflows here) or go all in and write your own code.

**What you need to know for now:** Stream Designer Studio is a graphical programming environment for low-code programs called streams.
